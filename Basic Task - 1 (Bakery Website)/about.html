Our Story

Cakey Bakers is a small bakery that makes fresh cakes, breads, cookies, cupcakes and other sweet items. We use good quality ingredients and bake everything with care.

Our goal is to provide fresh and tasty bakery products to our customers. We also make cakes for birthdays and special events.
